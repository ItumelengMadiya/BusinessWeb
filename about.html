About Us

Peters Business Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a small Accounting and Tax Services firm situated in Ennerdale, south of Johannesburg. The owner of the company, Mrs. Maldird Bernise Peters, has been an Accountant for four different companies over the past 25 years. In September 2016, she started working as an Accountant for a company, but it closed down after three months. Mrs. Peters then decided to pursue her passion for Accounting, and she established Peters Business Solutions in 2017.

Vision

To provide a professional Accounting and Tax service to small businesses in and around Johannesburg and Pretoria.

Mission

We strive for perfection and provide the highest standard of services to our clients, through the application of best practices and sustainably growing the profitability and value of the business.

Values

Integrity , Leadership , Service and Staff guides everything we do.
